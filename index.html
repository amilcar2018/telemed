FAQ’s

  • Do I need insurance to use the service?

    No. You do not need to have a health insurance plan to access our doctors and nurses 24/7. It's just like going to a walk-in urgent care or ER, except without all of the travel and high costs. And, if you need a prescription - they can write you one if they believe it to be medically needed. You'll also have 24/7 unlimited access to our Registered Nurse Line and Email-a-Doctor services at no additional cost.

  • Can I get a prescription?

    If medically needed. It is up to the doctor to recommend the best treatment. Teladoc® doctors do not issue prescriptions for substances controlled by the DEA, non-therapeutic, and/or certain other drugs which may be harmful because of their potential for abuse. Click here to view the current list of DEA controlled substances.

  • Is Hello Alvin® health insurance?

    No. Alvin Healthcare, Hello Alvin® and Alvin® are not health insurance, but we help many people who are either without an insurance plan, have a high-deductible health plan (HDHP) or want the convenience of not having to spend time traveling to an Urgent Care or ER for a minor illness for them or their family member. Anyone can use Hello Alvin®, whether insured or not.

  • Can I use Hello Alvin or Teladoc outside The United States?

    No. Hello Alvin® and Teladoc consults are not available outside of the United States.
